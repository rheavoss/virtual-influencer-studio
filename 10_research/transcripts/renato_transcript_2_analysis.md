## 2. The Loopholes & Sales Biases

### LOOPHOLE A: The Single Most Dangerous Contradiction (US Targeting via IP vs. Audience)

- **His Claim in Video #2:** "People think it depends on your IP address or the SIM card you use. It's none of that. It's simply a question of what audience watches and interacts with your content."
- **His Claim in Video #1:** "Always create accounts on a normal phone, no VPN, no proxies."
- **The Contradiction:** Both statements are partially true but create a false impression when combined. The IP address and SIM card at *account creation* establish the algorithm's initial geographic prior — what country it assumes the user is in. Early traction from that country then compounds. If you create the account in India with an Indian SIM, the first 100 people who see your boosted images will disproportionately be Indian if your boost audience targeting is weak. Once Indian users engage heavily, the algorithm locks you into Indian distribution for organic Reels.
- **The Real Truth:** Audience behavior determines long-term reach — but the starting geographic signal and the boosting targeting *determine what your early audience looks like*. Both matter.
- **Our Correction:** The safest approach is to create the account with a US residential mobile proxy OR a US SIM, AND ensure all ad boosts are explicitly geo-targeted to USA (Male, Age 22–45). Both levers must be set correctly, not just one.
