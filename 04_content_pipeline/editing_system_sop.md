## What Is It

A 6-step editing formula that turns raw AI video output into polished, publish-ready content fast — without creative burnout.

**Primary tool:** CapCut (free, mobile + desktop) or Premiere Pro for complex edits.

---

## The 6 Steps

### Step 1 — Auto-Cut
- Import raw clip into CapCut / Premiere Pro
- Use auto-cut on silence (removes dead air automatically)
- Target: clip should feel snappy from first frame

### Step 2 — Music First
- Choose music BEFORE editing visuals
- Music sets the pace — edit visuals to match beats
- Source: CapCut royalty-free library or Trending IG audio
- Fade in/out: 0.3 seconds each end

### Step 3 — Edit the Inspired Section First
- Find the strongest 3–5 seconds of the clip
- Edit that section to perfection first
- Build the rest of the video around that anchor
- Prevents starting with the hardest part (reduces burnout)

### Step 4 — Apply Templates
- Use a consistent Jasmine template:
  - Font: [locked in brand kit]
  - Text size: 60–80pt
  - Position: bottom third
  - Color: white with black drop shadow
- Template ensures visual consistency across all posts

### Step 5 — Sound Effects
- Add 1–2 sound effects per video (not more)
- Recommended effects:
  - Subtle "whoosh" on text appearance
  - Heartbeat or soft bass hit on hook frame
  - NO cheesy effects
- Source: CapCut sound effects library (free)

### Step 6 — Quick Rewatch
- Watch the full video 1× at 1.5x speed
- Check: does it feel too slow anywhere?
- Check: is the CTA clearly visible?
- Check: would you stop scrolling at frame 1?
- If any answer is no → fix only that section, nothing else

## Time Budget Per Video

| Video Type | Edit Time |
|---|---|
| 15-second IG Reel | 20–30 minutes |
| 30-second Reel | 35–45 minutes |
| 60-second Reel | 45–60 minutes |

If editing takes longer → the raw clip is bad, not the editor. Regenerate the source.
