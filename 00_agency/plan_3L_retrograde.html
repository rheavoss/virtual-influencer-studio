What Could Slow This Down
Know these before they happen
LoRA training delayed (current blocker)
Full-res images must be recovered from Kling history (legs cropped issue). Every week of training delay = 1 week pushed on M1 launch. CEO action required today.
No viral Reel in M1–M2
Organic growth stalls without at least 1 breakout Reel. Must batch 20+ Reels in pre-launch and A/B test hooks aggressively. Paid ads compensate but increase burn.

If conversion is 0.8%, you need 31k followers instead of 25k for ₹3L. Add ₹30k in extra ad spend. Mitigate: aggressive Fanvue CTA in Stories + link-in-bio A/B test.
IG account flagged / restricted
Risk window: first 30 days. Post only SFW content. No external adult links in bio until 1k followers. Use custom domain redirect (not Linktree) for Fanvue link.
Meta Ads CPM spike
India CPM for female lifestyle is ₹50–150. Budget built with ₹100 CPM assumption. If CPM doubles, slow the ad spend — organic content velocity compensates.
