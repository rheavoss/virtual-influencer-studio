## Native Platform Monetization — Real Data (April 2026)

| Platform | Unlocks At | Rate | Est. Monthly at 10k followers |
|---|---|---|---|
| **IG Subscriptions** | 10,000 followers | Creator sets $0.99–$99.99/month, IG takes 30% | ₹5,000–₹25,000 |
| **FB Subscriptions** | 10,000 followers | Patreon-style, FB takes 30% | ₹3,000–₹15,000 |
| **FB In-stream Ads** | 10,000 followers + 600k min watched/60 days | $3–$8 RPM (lifestyle niche) | ₹2,000–₹8,000 at 1M views |
| **FB Stars (tips)** | 10,000 followers | $0.01 per star | ₹500–₹3,000 |
| **YouTube Shorts** | 1,000 subs + YPP | $0.03–$0.08 per 1,000 views | ⚠️ Near zero — cross-post only |
| **YouTube Memberships** | YPP eligible | Creator keeps 70% | ₹3,000–₹10,000 at 500 members |

**Key insight Grok missed:** IG and FB subscriptions unlock at 10,000 followers — achievable Month 1–2 on the tech-leveraged timeline. This adds ₹8,000–₹40,000/month BEFORE Fanvue PPV is at scale.

## pSEO Reality Check

pSEO (P1-48, P1-49) is a **Year-2 revenue engine**, not Year-1:
- Pages built Month 2–3
- Google indexes Month 3–4
- First rankings Month 4–6
- Meaningful traffic (10k+/month) Month 6–9
- Revenue conversion Month 7+

Do NOT include pSEO in Year-1 revenue projections. Include it in Year-2 projections where it compounds.
