# Facebook Stars & Gifts Strategy
**Date:** 2026-04-11
**Target Character:** Sonia

## 1. Applicability (What can receive Stars?)
Initially, Stars were restricted to Live Streams and gaming. However, Meta has expanded this globally. As seen in your screenshot, **Facebook Stars are now formally applicable to:**
- Static Photo Posts (Images)
- Text Posts
- Facebook Reels
- Live Streams & Standard VODs (Video on Demand)

## 2. The Creator "Cost" (Revenue Share)
- **Creator Payout:** You earn a flat **$0.01 USD** (approx. ₹0.83 INR) per Star received. Meaning: 100 Stars = $1.00 USD.
- **The "Tax" on the Viewer:** In your screenshot, a viewer buys "29 Stars for ₹30.00". That means the viewer pays roughly ₹1.03 per Star. Facebook takes the ~20% spread as their transaction fee/app store fee, but your payout is always protected at a flat $0.01 USD.

## 3. Eligibility Requirements
To unlock Stars on your account, Jasmine's page must hit these gates:
- **Followers:** 500 followers maintained for 30 consecutive days.
- **Age:** The account owner (You) must be 18+.
- **Policy:** Must comply strictly with Meta's Partner Monetization Policies. *(This makes our Option A 'Edge-of-TOS' content ceiling highly important. If we cross into nudity, the page loses Stars eligibility instantly).*

## 4. Payout Frequency & Thresholds
- **The Threshold:** You must accumulate **$100 USD** (10,000 Stars) before Meta triggers a payout. *(Note: Meta has recently experimented with lowering this to $25 out of the US, but model your P&L on the $100 threshold).*
- **The Frequency:** Payouts happen once a month, issued **21 days** after the end of the month in which you hit the threshold. (e.g., Stars earned in May are paid out ~June 21st).

## 5. Tax Setup
Before Meta releases your first dollar of Stars, you will need to complete their Tax onboarding in the Meta Business Suite:
- **For India:** You will be required to submit a digital **W-8BEN form** (to prove you are a non-US resident) and supply your local PAN card details. 
- **Withholding:** Without the W-8BEN, Meta can hold up to 30% of US-sourced revenue. With a proper W-8BEN and India's tax treaty, the withholding tax on US tips drops significantly.

## Strategy Integration
We should treat Facebook Stars as a **secondary, passive revenue stream**, meant to offset your $5 VPS and ElevenLabs costs. The primary driver remains the Fanvue subscription via the Link-in-Bio, but activating Stars on Jasmine's daily SFW photo posts creates "free money" from older demographics who prefer not to leave Facebook.
